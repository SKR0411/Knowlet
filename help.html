Knowlet Help Center

Learn how to use Knowlet with these short tutorial videos.

Install the Knowlet Web App

This video shows how to install the Knowlet web app on your phone or computer. Installing the web app allows you to access notes faster and use Knowlet like a normal mobile app.

  • Open the Knowlet website in your browser
  • Tap the browser menu
  • Select Add to Home Screen
  • The Knowlet app icon will appear on your device

Create a Knowlet Account

In this tutorial you will learn how to create a Knowlet account. Having an account lets you save favourites, track your downloads, and access your notes easily.

  • Open the Knowlet website
  • Click the Sign Up button
  • Enter your name, email, and password
  • Submit the form to create your account

Download PDF Notes

This video explains how to find and download PDF notes from Knowlet. You can browse subjects, open a note, and download the PDF to your device for offline study.

  • Open the subject you want
  • Select the chapter or topic
  • Click the Download PDF button
  • The file will be saved to your device
