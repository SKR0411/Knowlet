Question 1 (Answer any four) 1 x 4 = 4 Marks

(a) What is pandemic?

[span_0](start_span)

A pandemic is an epidemic of an infectious disease that has spread across a large region, typically multiple continents or worldwide, affecting a substantial number of people[span_0](end_span).

(b) What is epidemic?

[span_1](start_span)

An epidemic is the rapid spread of a disease to a large number of people in a given population within a short period of time[span_1](end_span).

(c) What is disease outbreak?

[span_2](start_span)

A disease outbreak is a sudden rise in the number of cases of a disease in a particular place or time period[span_2](end_span).

(d) What is a parasite?

[span_3](start_span)

A parasite is an organism that lives on or in a host organism and gets its food from or at the expense of its host[span_3](end_span).

(e) What is endemic?

[span_4](start_span)

Endemic refers to a disease or condition regularly found among particular people or in a certain area[span_4](end_span).

Question 6 (Answer any one) 8 Marks

Life Cycle: The life cycle of a silkworm (e.g., Bombyx mori) consists of four stages: Egg -> Larva (Caterpillar) -> Pupa (inside Cocoon) -> Adult (Moth)[span_33](end_span).

[span_34](start_span)

Option B: (b) Discuss the culture of lac for commercial production in detail. Add a note on shellac. [span_36](start_span)

Lac Culture: Lac culture involves the inoculation of host plants (like Kusum, Ber, or Palas) with brood lac[span_36](end_span). [span_37](start_span)The larvae settle on twigs and secrete resin (lac) for protection[span_37](end_span). [span_38](start_span)Harvesting is done by scraping the encrustations from the twigs[span_38](end_span).

[span_39](start_span)

Shellac: Shellac is a refined form of lac, obtained by purifying seed lac[span_39](end_span). [span_40](start_span)It is widely used as a food glaze, wood finish, and in pharmaceutical coatings[span_40](end_span).

Exam Focus Enhancements

Exam Tips

  • For 8-mark questions, always provide a clear definition before starting the description.
  • In Economic Zoology, mentioning scientific names (underlined or in italics) adds significant value to your answers.

Common Mistakes

  • Confusing 'Epidemic' with 'Endemic'. Remember: Endemic is *regular*, Epidemic is a *sudden spread*.
  • Forgetting to mention the specific host plants for silkworms and lac insects.

Important Scientific Names List

  • Honeybee (Indian): Apis cerana indica
  • Lac Insect: Kerria lacca
  • Silkworm (Mulberry): Bombyx mori
  • Rohu: Labeo rohita
