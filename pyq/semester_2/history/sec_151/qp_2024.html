34. Write a note on Kathak dance. [5 Marks]

[span_41](start_span)

Kathak is one of the eight major forms of Indian classical dance[span_41](end_span).

    [span_42](start_span)
  • Origin: Its roots lie in the traveling bards of North India known as Kathakars, who told stories from epics using dance[span_42](end_span).
    • [span_43](start_span)
  • Features: It is characterized by rhythmic footwork (Tatkar), spectacular spins (Chakkars), and subtle facial expressions (Abhinaya)[span_43](end_span).
    • [span_44](start_span)
  • Gharanas: The dance evolved into three distinct schools or Gharanas: Lucknow, Jaipur, and Benares[span_44](end_span).
    • [span_45](start_span)
  • Exponents: Pandit Birju Maharaj is the most famous modern exponent of this form[span_45](end_span).
