Exam Focus Enhancements

Exam Tips

  • For 5-mark questions, always list points clearly using bullets or numbered steps.
  • When asked for differences (like Intranet vs Extranet), using a table is the best presentation strategy.

Common Mistakes

  • Writing AVG() in Excel instead of AVERAGE().
  • Confusing 'Slide Transition' with 'Animation' in PowerPoint (Transition is for the whole slide, Animation is for objects on the slide).

Important Formulas List

  • SUM(range): Adds all numbers in a range.
  • AVERAGE(range): Calculates the arithmetic mean.
  • COUNTIF(range, criteria): Counts cells that meet a single condition.
