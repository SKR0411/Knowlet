1. (c) Between formic acid and acetic acid, which one is more acidic and why? [2]

Formic acid (HCOOH) is more acidic than acetic acid (CH3COOH). This is because the methyl group in acetic acid has a +I (inductive) effect, which increases electron density on the carboxylate group, destabilizing the conjugate base. [span_4](start_span)Formic acid lacks this destabilizing effect[span_4](end_span).
