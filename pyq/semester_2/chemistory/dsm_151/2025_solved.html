Exam Preparation Toolkit

  • Uncertainty: Δx.Δp ≥ h/4π
  • RMS Velocity: v = sqrt(3RT/M)
  • Bond Order: 0.5(Nb - Na)
  • Huckel's Rule: 4n+2 π electrons
  • Poiseuille's: η = (πPr⁴t)/(8Vl)

Common Mistakes

  • Confusion between Square Planar (XeF4) and Tetrahedral geometry.
  • Forgetting to convert Temperature to Kelvin (K) in gas law numericals.
  • Incorrectly placing lone pairs in VSEPR structures (ClF3).

Presentation Tips

  • Always draw MO diagrams with labeled energy axes and orbital names (σ, π, σ*, π*).
  • In organic chemistry questions, use curved arrows to show electron movement.
