Exam Focus Enhancements

Exam Tips

  • Use Diagrams: Always draw the Bloom's Taxonomy pyramid or the tri-polar communication triangle in long answers.
  • Differentiate Clearly: Use tables for "Method vs. Approach" and "Analytic vs. Synthetic" questions.

Common Mistakes

  • Confusing the "Post-active phase" (evaluation) with the "Interactive phase" (actual teaching).
  • Mixing up Inductive (Example to Rule) and Deductive (Rule to Example) methods.

Important Concepts List

  • Maxims: Simple to Complex, Known to Unknown.
  • Bloom's Domains: Cognitive, Affective, Psychomotor.
  • Levels: Memory, Understanding, Reflective.

Answer Presentation Strategy

Begin every long answer with a standard definition of the core term. Use bullet points for merits/demerits and always write a concluding sentence that summarizes the utility of the method or concept discussed.
