Exam Success Strategy

Key Terms to Remember

  • Pramana: Valid source of knowledge.
  • Pratyaksa: Perception.
  • Avidya: Ignorance (the common cause of bondage).

Common Mistakes

  • Confusing the "Orthodox" (Vedic) schools with "Heterodox" (Jain, Buddhist, Carvaka).
  • Forgetting to mention the 12 links when explaining Pratityasamutpada.

Presentation Strategy

Use Sanskrit terminology alongside English translations. For 10-mark questions, always start with a clear definition and end with a summary of the school's impact.
