UNIT-I

Question 1 [2 x 2 = 4 Marks]

(a) Mention any two characteristics of a company.

    [span_5](start_span)
  • Separate Legal Entity: A company is a distinct legal person existing independent of its members.[span_5](end_span)
    • [span_6](start_span)
  • Perpetual Succession: The existence of a company is not affected by the death, insolvency, or retirement of its members.[span_6](end_span)

(b) What is meant by 'lifting of the corporate veil'?

[span_7](start_span)Lifting of the corporate veil refers to the legal concept where courts ignore the separate legal entity of a company to look at the individuals (members or directors) behind the corporate structure, typically in cases of fraud or improper conduct.[span_7](end_span)

(c) What is a pre-incorporation contract?

[span_8](start_span)

A pre-incorporation contract is a contract entered into by the promoters on behalf of a company before it has been formally incorporated or granted a certificate of registration.[span_8](end_span)

UNIT-II

Question 3 [2 x 2 = 4 Marks]

(a) Define Articles of Association.

[span_24](start_span)The Articles of Association (AoA) represent the document that contains the rules, regulations, and bye-laws for the internal management and conduct of the business of a company.[span_24](end_span)

(b) What is meant by 'forfeiture of shares'?

[span_25](start_span)

Forfeiture of shares occurs when a company cancels the shares held by a member due to their failure to pay call money within the specified period.[span_25](end_span)

(c) What are bonus shares?

Bonus shares are additional shares given to existing shareholders without any extra cost, based upon the number of shares that a shareholder owns. [span_26](start_span)These are issued out of the company's accumulated profits or reserves.[span_26](end_span)

Option B

What is buy-back of shares? Discuss its legal provisions.

[span_35](start_span)

Buy-back is a process where a company purchases its own shares from the existing shareholders to reduce the number of shares outstanding in the open market.[span_35](end_span)

Legal Provisions (Section 68 of Companies Act, 2013):

    [span_36](start_span)
  • Sources: Buy-back can be done from free reserves, securities premium account, or proceeds of any previous issue.[span_36](end_span)
    • [span_37](start_span)
  • Authorization: Must be authorized by the AoA and a special resolution in a general meeting.[span_37](end_span)
    • [span_38](start_span)
  • Limits: Buy-back cannot exceed 25% of the aggregate of paid-up capital and free reserves.[span_38](end_span)
    • [span_39](start_span)
  • Debt-Equity Ratio: Post buy-back ratio should not exceed 2:1.[span_39](end_span)
    • [span_40](start_span)
  • Physical Destruction: Shares bought back must be physically destroyed within 7 days.[span_40](end_span)

UNIT-III

Question 5 [2 x 2 = 4 Marks]

(a) Who is an independent director?

[span_41](start_span)

An independent director is a non-executive director who does not have any material or pecuniary relationship with the company or its promoters, ensuring unbiased decision-making.[span_41](end_span)

(b) Define key managerial personnel (KMP).

[span_42](start_span)

KMP refers to a group of people who are in charge of maintaining the operations of the organization, such as the CEO, Managing Director, Company Secretary, and CFO.[span_42](end_span)

(c) How is a director removed from office?

[span_43](start_span)

A director can be removed by the shareholders passing an Ordinary Resolution at a general meeting after giving a special notice, as per Section 169.[span_43](end_span)

Question 9 [2 x 2 = 4 Marks]

(a) Define insider trading.

[span_74](start_span)Insider trading is the illegal practice of trading on the stock exchange to one's own advantage through having access to confidential or non-public price-sensitive information.[span_74](end_span)

(b) What is the role of a liquidator in a company?

[span_75](start_span)

A liquidator is an officer appointed to wind up the affairs of a company by realizing its assets, paying off debts, and distributing the remaining balance to members.[span_75](end_span)

    • [span_77](start_span)
