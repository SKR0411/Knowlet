[span_15](start_span)[span_16](start_span)

Alauddin Khilji's economic and market control policies were designed primarily to maintain a large standing army at low cost[span_15](end_span)[span_16](end_span):

    [span_17](start_span)
  • Price Fixation: He strictly fixed the prices of all essential commodities, from food grains to cloth and even slaves[span_17](end_span).
    • [span_18](start_span)
  • Market Regulations: Three separate markets were established in Delhi for food grains, expensive cloth, and horses/slaves[span_18](end_span).
    • [span_19](start_span)
  • The Shahna-i-Mandi: Each market was placed under a high officer called the Shahna-i-Mandi to ensure compliance and prevent hoarding[span_19](end_span).
    • [span_20](start_span)
  • Rationing System: During times of scarcity, a rationing system was implemented where each household was allowed a specific amount of grain[span_20](end_span).
    • [span_21](start_span)
  • Taxation: He increased land revenue to 50% of the produce and introduced new taxes like Ghari (house tax) and Charai (pasture tax)[span_21](end_span).

Question 2(b) [Long Answer] [10 Marks]

Write a note on the Continental System of Napoleon.

[span_43](start_span)

The Continental System was Napoleon's attempt to defeat Britain by paralyzing its economy[span_43](end_span):

    [span_44](start_span)
  • Economic Blockade: Napoleon prohibited all European nations under his control from trading with Britain[span_44](end_span).
    • [span_45](start_span)
  • Berlin and Milan Decrees: These decrees declared the British Isles under blockade and ordered the seizure of any ship touching a British port[span_45](end_span).
    • [span_46](start_span)
  • Objective: The goal was to destroy British commerce and industry, leading to internal unrest and eventual surrender[span_46](end_span).
    • [span_47](start_span)
  • Failure: The system failed because of British naval superiority, large-scale smuggling, and the dependence of European countries on British goods[span_47](end_span).
    • [span_48](start_span)
  • Backfire: It alienated Napoleon's allies and directly led to the Peninsular War and the disastrous invasion of Russia[span_48](end_span).
