[span_15](start_span)

Epistasis: A phenomenon where the effect of one gene (epistatic gene) masks or modifies the expression of another gene (hypostatic gene) at a different locus[span_15](end_span).

[span_16](start_span)

Difference from Dominance: Dominance involves interaction between alleles at the same locus, whereas epistasis involves interaction between genes at different loci[span_16](end_span).

Supplementary Gene Interaction: Occurs when the dominant allele of one gene produces a character on its own, but the dominant allele of a second gene cannot produce its own character unless the first gene is present.
