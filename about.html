Knowlet

A Smart and Simple Learning Companion

What is Knowlet?

Knowlet is a modern web-based learning platform designed to help students organize notes, access study materials, and improve productivity. It focuses on simplicity, speed, and offline support through modern web technologies.

When Was It Developed?

Knowlet was developed between September and October 2025 as a personal project, and it was officially released on 5 December 2025 (Friday) with the domain knowlet.in. It was built with the goal of creating a lightweight and installable web application that works smoothly even on low-resource devices.

Why Was It Developed?

Many college students struggle with scattered notes, notes from different sources, and websites that require constant internet access. Knowlet was created to solve these problems by offering:

  • Managed notes like a digital library
  • Fast loading experience
  • Offline support
  • Simple navigation
  • Organized semester and subject structure
  • Minimal and distraction-free design
  • Separate favourite notes section

Who Developed It?

Knowlet was developed by Sujan Roy, an independent student developer passionate about building efficient web applications. The platform was built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Progressive Web App (PWA) technologies.
